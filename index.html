A look inside

As I type out the title of the page "My Favorite Thing", I am reminded of my childhood, watching The Sound of Music and singing along to a song. A simple song about simple things that bring Maria Von Trapp joy. It has a sweet melody, and in the video we watch as an otherwise mundain night turns into chaos, but through the power of improve and the desire to wrap her newly designated wards in a atmosphere of comfort and normality, Maria beings to sing. She sings of comforts that like a chair in a room can blend into the landscape of our everyday, raindrops on roses and whiskers on kittens. Soon, there is dancing, giggling, and the Von Trapp children are rid of any fear or dread being replaced by joy. But, for Maria, who was struggling with acceptance in her new position, she, by giving to the children found joy herself.

Having grown and leaving behind the simplicity of childhood, we become hardened, skeptical, and out of obligation live for ourselves. In an attempt to re-kindle joy, we find things that bring us happiness. Happiness though, is fleeting, and joy at times seems like hope. Lost. What I have discovered through the path of turbulant times is giving. Giving, I find, will leave you overflowing with peace, true joy, hope, and love. As a bonus, giving when your strength is stretched so far it's transparent, is like drinking out of a firehose of joy. I encourage you to try it. Below are some ways I refill myself in giving back, I hope you find them inspiring.
